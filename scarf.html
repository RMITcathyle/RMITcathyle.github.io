sporky walked into the market and approached the scarf stall

The scarf stall had such a large variety of scarves in all different colours, materials and sizes. Sporky is starting to get overwhelmed about all the different choices and does not know which scarf to choose. Can you help Sporky choose a scarf to purchase?
The scarf options are a sky blue satin scarf ($40.50), a red sheeps wool scarf ($35.00), a caramel cashmere scarf ($80.00) or a rainbow cotton scarf ($45.00).


Artwork and dialogue is inspired by Disney Pixar's Forky from Toy Story 4
