sporky

Sporky needs to travel to the market to purchase a scarf however my tiny little legs cannot prolong for long distances therefore I must utilise another means of transportation. The available options are a shiny, dazzling yellow car , a cute, resilient, sturdy camel or a small but fiesty scooter.

Analysing the code, decide which option is the most time efficient for sporky.


Artwork and dialogue is inspired by Disney Pixar's Forky from Toy Story 4
